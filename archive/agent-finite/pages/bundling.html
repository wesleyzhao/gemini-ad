Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Gemini bundles.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What's included in the free trial?

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start, and you can cancel anytime during the trial period.

How does family sharing work?

Premium plans include sharing for up to 5 family members. Each member gets their own account with full access to all Premium features while maintaining privacy and separate storage.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All plans include Google's industry-leading security with encryption in transit and at rest. Enterprise plans add advanced security controls, compliance certifications, and dedicated support.

What if I need more storage?

You can add additional storage to any plan starting at $1.99/month for 100 GB. Premium plans include 2 TB, and Complete plans offer unlimited storage for your entire organization.
